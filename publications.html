Working Papers & Field Reports

Working Paper · 2026

Pre-Threshold Governance: Legal Architecture for the Window of Intervention

Working Paper

Analysis of existing legal frameworks for evidence of pre-threshold governance logic. Identifies structural gaps and proposes architectural principles for the c-ECO institutional design layer.

First-cycle governance diagnostic from the Amazon Living Lab. Maps institutional authority, identifies critical governance gaps, and assesses threshold proximity in the study zone.
