// TFP Band System

Graduated activation

Legal effects scale continuously with systemic state. No binary on/off trigger — graduated activation proportional to remaining reversibility margin.

GREEN
Routine monitoring
P < 0.5 · Pcrit
Quarterly reporting. No escalation required.
AMBER
Precautionary activation
0.5 ≤ P/Pcrit < 0.85
Enhanced reporting. Precautionary obligations active.
RED
Trigger execution
P/Pcrit ≥ 0.85
Restoration fund release. Solve et repete operative.
BLACK
Financial finality
Threshold breached
Full guarantee conversion. No discretion.
