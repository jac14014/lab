// c-ECO Institute

Research infrastructure for anticipatory governance.

The c-ECO Institute advances scientific, educational, and charitable work on ecological governance, systemic risk, climate adaptation, disaster risk reduction, artificial intelligence, and public policy innovation.

Purpose

Before collapse

c-ECO develops methods, documents, training, and institutional tools that help public-interest actors recognize thresholds before ecological and social systems cross points of irreversibility.

Method

Science to instrument

The platform links Threshold Dynamics Recognition, the Threshold Function Protocol, legal information systems, and living laboratories into a practical governance architecture.

Public benefit

Education and research

The Institute supports research publication, fellowships, technical assistance, capacity building, and international cooperation aligned with nonprofit public benefit purposes.

Governance

Institutional clarity

Its governance separates board oversight, executive leadership, technical integrity, compliance, and programme delivery into transparent portfolios.
